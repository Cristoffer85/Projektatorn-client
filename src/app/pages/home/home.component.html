Welcome to Projektatorn!


Ask the prompt below what type of project you would like to co-work on with your friends - and gemini api collects and returns these parameters to you a list of 10 possible projects. You can then select two ideas from this list and send to your friends.

The goal of this application is co-creation for raising your attraction to future employers and brush off teamworking skills (and what do you know maybe some good friends too?)
They can then reply what project sounds most interesting - and bam you can set up your project together where you choose to start co-creating!
